Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, Download the app or through web, access the SignIn/SignUp in the navigation bar or Settings.

Minting an NFT on VaultX is easy. Follow these steps:
  1. Go to the "Register Asset" section
  2. Upload your digital asset
  3. Fill out the required metadata
  4. Confirm the transaction
Your NFT will be minted on the XRP Ledger and stored in your Wallet.

You can download the VaultX white paper from the "About" page. Simply click the "Download White Paper" button to get the PDF version.

Yes, you can transfer your Assets from VaultX to any compatible VaultX user. Follow these steps:
  1. Go to the "Wallet" section
  2. Click "Transfer"
  3. Select the Asset you wish to transfer
  4. Enter the recipient's wallet address

To connect your XUMM Wallet to VaultX:
  1. Click on the "Connect Wallet" button at the top of the dashboard
  2. You will be prompted to log in using your XUMM Wallet app
  3. Your account will be linked automatically

If you forget your password:
  • Reset it through the XUMM app by following the password recovery instructions
  • Note that VaultX does not store your XUMM Wallet password
  • You will need to reset it directly via the XUMM app

The XRP cryptocurrency, designed for cross-border settlements.
